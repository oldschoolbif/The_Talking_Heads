# FLAME 2023 Open vs FLAME 2023 Standard

**Date:** November 2025  
**Purpose:** Choose between FLAME 2023 Open and FLAME 2023 Standard

---

## Quick Answer

**Recommendation: FLAME 2023 Open** ⭐

**Why:**
- ✅ **CC-BY-4.0 license** - Commercial use allowed
- ✅ **Same improvements** - Revised eye region, improved expressions
- ✅ **Simpler** - One version (vs multiple versions)
- ✅ **Better for your use case** - Podcast project may need commercial use

---

## Comparison

| Feature | FLAME 2023 Open | FLAME 2023 Standard |
|---------|-----------------|---------------------|
| **License** | ✅ CC-BY-4.0 (commercial use) | ⚠️ Research license (limited commercial) |
| **Eye Region** | ✅ Revised | ✅ Revised |
| **Expressions** | ✅ Improved | ✅ Improved |
| **Jaw Rotation** | ✅ Included | ✅ Options (with/without) |
| **Versions** | ✅ Single version | ⚠️ Multiple versions (with/without jaw) |

## Detailed Differences

### FLAME 2023 Open (CC-BY-4.0)

**Pros:**
- ✅ **Commercial use allowed** - Important for podcast projects
- ✅ **CC-BY-4.0 license** - Very permissive
- ✅ **Same improvements** - Revised eye region, improved expressions
- ✅ **Simpler** - One version to download
- ✅ **Future-proof** - No licensing concerns

**Cons:**
- ⚠️ May not have separate jaw rotation versions (but jaw rotation is included)

**Best for:**
- ✅ Commercial projects
- ✅ Podcast/video production
- ✅ Projects that may be monetized
- ✅ Simpler setup (one version)

---

### FLAME 2023 Standard

**Pros:**
- ✅ **Latest features** - Revised eye region, improved expressions
- ✅ **Jaw rotation options** - Versions with/without jaw rotation
- ✅ **More control** - Can choose specific version

**Cons:**
- ❌ **Limited commercial use** - Research license restrictions
- ⚠️ **Multiple versions** - Need to choose which one
- ⚠️ **License complexity** - May need to check terms

**Best for:**
- ✅ Research projects
- ✅ Academic use
- ✅ Projects needing specific jaw rotation options
- ✅ Non-commercial projects

## What About Jaw Rotation?

**FLAME 2023 Standard** mentions "versions w/ and w/o jaw rotation"

**Question:** Do you need jaw rotation control?

**Answer:**
- ✅ **FLAME 2023 Open** includes jaw rotation (it's part of FLAME 2023)
- ✅ **DECA** handles jaw rotation automatically
- ⚠️ **Separate versions** in FLAME 2023 Standard just let you choose if you want jaw rotation or not

**For DECA:**
- DECA uses FLAME's jaw rotation automatically
- You don't need to choose - DECA handles it
- FLAME 2023 Open is fine (includes jaw rotation)
