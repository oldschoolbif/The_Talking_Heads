# D-ID Check: Super Simple Version

## 🎯 Goal
Check if your D-ID account has AWS credentials available.

---

## ⚡ 3-Minute Quick Check

### Step 1: Open D-ID Website
1. Open your browser
2. Go to: **studio.d-id.com**
3. Log in if needed

### Step 2: Find Settings
1. Look at **top-right corner** of the page
2. Click on your **name** or **profile picture** (👤)
3. Click **"Settings"** in the menu

### Step 3: Find API Section
1. Look for a tab or link that says **"API"**
2. Click on it

### Step 4: Look for "AWS"
1. On the API page, look for the word **"AWS"**
2. Or look for **"Access Key"** or **"Secret Key"**
3. Use browser search: Press **Ctrl+F**, type "AWS", press Enter

### Step 5: Report Back
**Tell me:**
- ✅ "I found AWS credentials" OR
- ❌ "I don't see AWS anywhere" OR
- 🤔 "I see something but I'm not sure"
